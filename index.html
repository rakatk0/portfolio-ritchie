About me

Hello, I'm Ritchie. I've got a Landscape Architecture degree from the University of Kentucky (2006), where my mapping career began. After a decade in New Orleans doing mapping, planning, design and assorted freelancery, I moved back to Kentucky, where currently I'm helping to establish an Unmanned Systems Technology (drone) program at Maysville Community and Technical College. As such, I can often be found experimenting with potentially unsafe drone configurations in sparsely populated areas.
